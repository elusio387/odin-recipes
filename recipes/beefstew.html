Beef stew

Description

This is a recipe for a beef stew ladled with veggies, stock and herbs. It will take approximately 2 hours to cook, using a beef chuck stew meat with enough fat as a lean meat will dry out.

Ingredients

  • chuck beef
  • olive oil
  • carrots
  • potatoes
  • beef broth
  • celery
  • tomato paste
  • water

Steps

  1. Heat oil in pan and brown beef.
  2. Remove beef and set aside.
  3. Add onions to the pot until softened
  4. Add beef broth
  5. Add browned beef, potatoes, celery, tomato paste, and carrots
  6. Simmer for 1 hour on medium-low
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste
