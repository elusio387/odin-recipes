Beef Pho

Description

This is a recipe for a beef pho. It originated in Vietnam and has gained popularity being one of the best noodle soups in the world. It combines beef, noodles, and a bunch of herbs with the broth coming out as amazing to taste.

Ingredients

  • Beef brisket
  • onions, ginger
  • star anise, cinnamon, cardamon, coriander seeds
  • sugar, salt, fish sauce
  • toppings - beansprouts, thai basil, coriander, lime, hoisin sauce

Steps

  1. Make the broth
  2. Strain the broth
  3. Cook the noodles
  4. Cook the beef
  5. Combine and serve
